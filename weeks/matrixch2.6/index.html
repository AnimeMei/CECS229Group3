Def: Convex Combination

An expression of the form

αu⃗+βv⃗\alpha \; \vec{u} + \beta \; \vec{v}α​u​⃗​​+β​v​⃗​​
where
α,β≥0\alpha \; , \beta \; \geq 0α,β≥0
α+β=1\alpha \; + \; \beta = 1α+β=1
is called a convex combination of u⃗\vec{u}​u​⃗​​ and v⃗\vec{v}​v​⃗​​.
